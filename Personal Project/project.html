Sun Safety Awareness Campaign
UVGuard launched a community awareness campaign focused on teaching people about the dangers of excessive UV exposure. Through posters, social media content, and school presentations, the project educated students and families about the importance of sunscreen, protective clothing, and early skin checks. The campaign helped spread knowledge about preventing skin cancer and encouraged healthier habits during sunny seasons.

Free Skin Check Initiative
The Free Skin Check Initiative was created to encourage early detection of skin cancer. UVGuard worked with local health professionals to organize free skin examination events for the community. The project aimed to help people recognize unusual skin changes and understand when to seek medical advice. By promoting early detection, the initiative helped raise awareness and support healthier communities.
